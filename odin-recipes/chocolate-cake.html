A Chocolate Cake

This recipe will guide you in making

the best Chocolate Cake in the world.

Ingredients

  • Flower
  • Chocolate Powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Step by step guide to make a delicious Chocolate Cake

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200Cº
  2. Add 250g of Flower
  3. Add 5 eggs
  4. Add 500g of Milk
  5. Mix everything up
  6. Pour it to you favorite mold
  7. Take it to te oven for 45min
